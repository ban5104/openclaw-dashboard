# Compliance

## Claim Discipline
- Prefer quantifiable, contextual claims.
- Include baseline, outcome, and timeframe when possible.
- Mark estimates as estimates.
- Avoid universal guarantees.

## Hard Rules
- No fabricated case studies or testimonials.
- No deceptive or unverifiable outcome claims.
- Keep language accurate and non-hype.
- Use this disclaimer pattern when needed: "Results vary by process quality, team adoption, and implementation scope."
