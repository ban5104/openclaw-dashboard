# Avoided Posts

Avoid these patterns:

1. **AI theater**
   - Tool-name salad, hype, or fear-of-missing-out framing.
   - No clear audience, mechanism, or business outcome.

2. **Unverified proof content**
   - Invented stats, invented testimonials, or implied guarantees.

3. **Generic cross-post sludge**
   - Copy that reads like the same post pasted to every platform.
