# Winning Posts

Use these patterns:

1. **Mechanism explainer**
   - Start with an everyday SME bottleneck.
   - Explain the process failure in plain language.
   - Show how automation fixes the bottleneck without overcomplicating it.

2. **Local authority post**
   - Anchor to Nelson–Tasman business reality.
   - Use practical insight over trend-chasing.
   - Finish with a simple CTA like requesting a workflow audit.

3. **ROI-framed post**
   - Lead with time saved, handoffs improved, or admin reduced.
   - Include caveats where evidence is limited.
   - Avoid absolute outcome claims.
