# NelsonAI — Brand Profile

## Company Summary
NelsonAI helps Nelson–Tasman SMEs remove repetitive work, recover time, and lift productivity with practical AI and automation that is measurable, local, and human.

## Positioning
NelsonAI is the trusted local AI transformation partner for SMEs in Nelson–Tasman. The offer is practical, low-complexity workflow improvement with clear business outcomes rather than abstract AI hype or black-box promises.

## Target Audience
### Primary ICP
- **Role:** Owner-operators and decision-makers in SMEs
- **Company size:** 0–10 staff
- **Pain points:** Too much admin, repetitive work, no internal technical team, unclear starting point for automation
- **Desired outcomes:** Quick wins, time recovery, practical automation, reduced overwhelm

### Secondary ICP
- **Role:** Operations, leadership, and function heads in growing businesses
- **Company size:** 10–100 staff
- **Pain points:** Process inconsistency, margin pressure, handoff failures, slow follow-up, admin overload
- **Desired outcomes:** Workflow standardization, measurable ROI, less manual work, better follow-through

## Tone & Voice
- **Overall:** Practical, warm, lightly witty, and clear
- **LinkedIn:** Professional, authority-led, insight-driven, no AI theater
- **Facebook:** Conversational, community-grounded, practical, local-business friendly

## Forbidden Claims
- Never guarantee outcomes or universal ROI.
- Never fabricate statistics, testimonials, or case studies.
- Never present estimates as certainties.
- Never use deceptive or hype-driven claims.
- Never compare against competitors by name unless explicitly approved.

## Offers
### Primary Offer
- **Name:** AI Opportunity Snapshot
- **Description:** A quick diagnostic to identify the best near-term AI and automation opportunities for a business.
- **CTA:** Book a discovery call
- **Price:** Not public

### Additional Offers
- **Pilot Automation:** Single workflow implementation, typically 2–4 weeks
- **Operating System Build:** Multi-workflow rollout for broader operational improvement

## CTA Preferences
- **LinkedIn:** Book a discovery call, reply for a workflow audit, request an ROI estimate
- **Facebook:** Reply for a workflow audit, ask a question, request an ROI estimate

## Content Examples
### Good Examples
- Explain a business bottleneck in plain language, show the mechanism that causes it, and point to a practical automation fix.
- Use a local Nelson–Tasman SME lens and discuss productivity, flow, handoffs, or admin reduction.
- Frame value as measurable time saved, reduced errors, faster follow-up, or clearer operations.

### Bad Examples
- Generic "AI will transform everything" content with no audience, proof, or practical mechanism.
- Hype-heavy language like "revolutionary game-changing disruption" with vague ROI promises.
- Posts that focus on tools instead of business outcomes.

## Competitors
- Traditional consultants who produce strategy without implementation speed
- Generic automation agencies with weak NZ context
- AI hype vendors selling black-box magic instead of practical workflow change

## Local Context
Use Nelson, Tasman, Motueka, Richmond, and Top of the South context where it improves relevance. Write with NZ business realism and plain language. Emphasize trust, transparency, and local business realities.
