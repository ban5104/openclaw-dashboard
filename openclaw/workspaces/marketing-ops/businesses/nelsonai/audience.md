# Audience

## ICPs
### Owner-led SMEs
- 0–10 staff
- Time-poor and process-heavy
- Little or no internal technical team
- Need clarity and practical micro-automation

### Growing SMEs
- 10–100 staff
- Dealing with process inconsistency and margin pressure
- Need workflow standardization and admin reduction

## Vertical Focus
- Horticulture and post-harvest
- Manufacturing and engineering QA
- Professional services such as legal, accounting, and consulting

## Recurring Pain Points
- Too much admin and shadow work
- Inconsistent handoffs
- Slow follow-up and lead leakage
- Team overload and margin pressure
